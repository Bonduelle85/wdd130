Krasnaya Polyana

Book Now

Krasnaya Polyana is a popular ski resort located in the picturesque Caucasus Mountains of Russia. With its unique mountain scenery, abundance of snow and modern ski slopes, Krasnaya Polyana is an ideal destination for winter fun lovers. The resort offers a wide variety of trails for skiers of all skill levels, from beginners to advanced athletes. Here you will find several kilometers of well-prepared trails offering excellent skiing conditions. In addition, the resort offers an extensive network of ski lifts, which provide convenience and quick access to all slopes. Krasnaya Polyana also has many opportunities for freeriding, snow safaris and other types of winter activities.

You can rent equipment directly from the resort or hire an instructor to improve your skiing or snowboarding skills. In addition to winter sports, Krasnaya Polyana also has ski schools where children and adults can learn to ski or snowboard under the guidance of experienced instructors. The resort also offers a wide range of off-slope activities. You can enjoy snowmobiling, rope rides or visit cafes and restaurants serving national and international cuisine. Fresh mountain air, magnificent views of the surrounding nature and the opportunity to forget about everyday worries make a trip to Krasnaya Polyana an unforgettable experience that will delight skiers and snowboarders of any level.
