Sheregesh is one of the most popular and sought-after ski resorts in Russia. It is located in the Kemerovo region, in the Altai Mountains, in the heart of Siberia and offers a wide range of entertainment for winter sports lovers. The main feature of Sheregesh is its unique mountain, known as the "Mountain Range". With slopes up to 1,320 meters high, the resort offers a variety of pistes and slopes suitable for both beginners and experienced skiers and snowboarders. There are also modern lifts, including chairlifts, providing fast and comfortable lifting power. Sheregesh has a well-developed infrastructure. The resort offers a wide selection of hotels, cafes and restaurants where you can relax and have a snack after active skiing.

There is also ski equipment rental and professional instructors ready to help beginners master and improve their skills. One of the main attractions of Sheregesh is "Bolshaya Utyuganka" - a ski complex where various competitions are held, including the Snowboard World Cup. Various festivals, concerts and other entertainment events are also held here every year. The Sheregesh resort is actively developing and attracts more and more tourists with its excellent conditions for ski holidays. Thanks to its accessibility, beautiful nature and variety of entertainment, Sheregesh is considered one of the best ski resorts in the country.
